Youth Public Health Collaborative (YPHC)

We expand access to meaningful public health opportunities by building student-led teams in research, advocacy & education, and media — so motivated students can create real impact even when traditional STEM programs are expensive, limited, or extremely competitive.

What We Do

YPHC runs teams that build real deliverables — not busy work. Members learn by doing.

Research

Student-led research collaborations in public health — literature review, data analysis, writing, and project development.

Advocacy & Education

Campaigns, workshops, and community education designed to improve health literacy and create measurable outreach outcomes.

Media & Outreach

Design, social media, storytelling, and partnerships that expand reach and make public health messages clear and shareable.

Why YPHC Exists

Opportunity shouldn’t depend on money or luck.

Many STEM and research opportunities are expensive, geographically limited, or have extremely low acceptance rates. YPHC creates a structured pathway where students can grow skills, build a portfolio, and contribute to public health — while keeping access open.
