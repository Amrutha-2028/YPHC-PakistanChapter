# Youth Public Health Collaborative

### Empowering the next generation of public health leaders.

Access to meaningful STEM and research opportunities is often limited by high costs, limited seats, or extremely competitive acceptance rates. The Youth Public Health Collaborative (YPHC) was founded to help bridge this gap — creating accessible, student-led opportunities that allow young leaders to contribute to meaningful public health initiatives while gaining real-world experience.

---

## Our Mission
We aim to expand access to research, advocacy, and education opportunities for students worldwide while working to improve community public health outcomes through youth-driven innovation.

---

## Our Pillars

### Research
Student-led research collaborations addressing public health challenges through data, literature review, and innovative project development.

### Advocacy & Education
Public awareness campaigns, educational initiatives, and policy-focused outreach designed to improve community health literacy.

### Media & Outreach
Creative media campaigns, global outreach initiatives, and strategic partnerships to expand impact.

---

## Our Impact
- Multi-state and international membership  
- Student-led leadership structure  
- Ongoing public health research and education initiatives  
- Global collaboration opportunities
