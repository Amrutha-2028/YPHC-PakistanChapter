# About YPHC

The Youth Public Health Collaborative (YPHC) is a student-led global initiative dedicated to expanding access to research, advocacy, and education opportunities in public health.

Many students interested in STEM and healthcare face significant barriers — including expensive programs, geographic limitations, or extremely competitive opportunities with limited acceptance rates. YPHC was founded to create accessible pathways for students to gain meaningful experience while making measurable contributions to community health.

---

## Our Vision
A world where every motivated student has access to meaningful opportunities to contribute to improving public health.
